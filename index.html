Robert's Baked Goods

Freshly baked American doughnuts and bagels

'Making the world go round'

Robert's Baked Goods was founded in 2023 by owner and operator Robert Pinney. Originally from upstate New York, USA, Robert moved to Norway at the age of 25 and brought with him a zeal for making American baked goods. Robert first started making doughnuts for campers at an upscale vacation resort in 1999 and after many years of various employment found his way back to his original passion.
